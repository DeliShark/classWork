Study Tips

Below are a few tips to help students succeed in their study endeavors.

    Tips

  1. Pomodoro
    Use the Pomodoro technique. Break your tasks into small chunks of focus time followed by breaks.

  2. "Study with Me"
    Find streamers on Youtube and Twitch who create a learning environment that you can jump in and out of without leaving home.

  3. Take care of your health
    Remember to hydrate and stretch during your breaks, keep your snacks healthy and get plenty of rest.

  4. Use the Feynman Technique
    Breakdown your topic until you have the ability to explain it to others, simply.
    For more information on the Feynman technique, click here for an article at collegeinfogeek.com.
